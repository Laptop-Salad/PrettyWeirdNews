Larry Appreciation Report

Photo of Larry from unnamed source - date between 2020-2022.

The Beginning

Sources state that Larry was first sighted around the years 2020 - 2022 in an English classroom in a school that cannot be named for privacy reasons. Larry was placed at the front of the classroom held up by a piece of tape.


We interviewed an ex-student of the unnamed school and they stated that a class was reading a book called “Holes”. Based on our research we suspect the book was “Holes” by Louis Sachar. In the book onions are mentioned repeatedly in relation to a character called “Sam”.

The teacher of said class then printed out a picture of an onion and the class decided on the name “Larry”.

Larry’s Personality

Larry is described as so incredibly lazy and one person we spoke to called him “the most useless class assistant”. However the person also stated that “we still love him for who he is” and “because he’s Larry”.

Another person we spoke to noted that “I once knew of a Larry the onion” which clearly shows the impact that Larry the Onion had left despite it being several years since his last sighting.

During the time Larry was sighted the teacher who first saw him stated that Larry was the laziest substitute teacher that had to have people aid him in breathing despite the fact that he is more than capable of it.

Final Notes

It is clear that Larry is well beloved and cherished onion despite his negative personality traits. He will forever be remembered in our hearts and the hearts of those who knew him.

Amanda 12/02/2024
